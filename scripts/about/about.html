A tribute to the witty and humorous lyrics of the seminal Deltron 3030.

Back in the great Winter of 2010 the great Drillionaire and yours truly rocked Deltron 3030 on repeat constantly.

The album – with its effortless beats, seamless samplings, and intelligent (and sometimes ridiculous) rhymes – slowly worked its way into our hearts.

Tribute!
